Our Evolution to Publication Automation

From clinical programming services to TLF Code Generator - 12+ years of expertise now focused on automating the path from protocol to publication.

The Journey to TLF Automation

Zinger Software Solutions began in 2013 with a simple mission: provide high-quality clinical programming services to biotechnology and pharmaceutical companies. Over 12 years, we've delivered thousands of successful SDTM datasets, ADaM analyses, and TLF outputs for studies ranging from early-phase biotech trials to large global submissions.

The Publication Challenge: After years of creating Tables, Listings, and Figures manually for our clients, we realized that 70-80% of TLF programming follows predictable patterns that could be automated while maintaining the quality and regulatory compliance our clients depend on.

This insight led to the development of ZinClin TLF Code Generator - an AI-powered platform that automates the creation of publication-ready deliverables. Instead of weeks of manual programming, biostatisticians and programming teams can now generate compliant TLFs in hours.

Our transition from services to SaaS platform represents the natural evolution of our expertise. We're not abandoning our roots - we're scaling our knowledge to help the entire industry accelerate from protocol to publication.
