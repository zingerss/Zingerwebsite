Our Story

Zinger Software Solutions began in 2013 with a simple mission: provide high-quality clinical programming services to biotechnology and pharmaceutical companies. Over 12 years, we've delivered thousands of successful SDTM datasets, ADaM analyses, and TLF outputs for studies ranging from early-phase biotech trials to large global submissions.

The Pattern We Keep Seeing: In every complex industry, practitioners spend enormous time on work that follows predictable patterns. We build the AI tools that automate those patterns — starting with clinical programming, and now expanding into financial markets.

This insight led to the development of ZinClin TLF Code Generator — an AI-powered platform that automates the creation of publication-ready Tables, Listings, and Figures. Instead of weeks of manual programming, clinical teams can now generate compliant TLFs in hours.

In 2025, we applied the same approach to financial markets. Traders and investors face a data overload problem — thousands of securities, endless indicators, and no systematic way to surface the few genuine opportunities that match their criteria. ZinTrade brings the same practitioner-first philosophy: built by people who trade, tested against real market data, transparent in its methodology.

Zinger Software Solutions is now a multi-product AI software company. We remain a focused team with deep expertise in our chosen domains. Our competitive advantage is not the size of our engineering team — it is the practitioner knowledge embedded in every product we build.

Company Evolution

2013

Company Founded

Zinger Software Solutions established as a boutique clinical programming consultancy serving biotech and pharmaceutical companies with SDTM, ADaM, and TLF services.

2018

TLF Expertise Recognition

Became recognized as specialists in publication-ready TLF creation, developing standardized processes that consistently delivered regulatory-compliant outputs ahead of submission deadlines.

2022

Automation Research Begins

Started research into AI-powered automation for TLF generation after identifying repeatable patterns across hundreds of client deliverables. Initial feasibility studies showed 60-70% efficiency gains were achievable.

2024

ZinClin Development Launched

Began full development of ZinClin TLF Code Generator platform, combining our 12+ years of hands-on TLF creation experience with cutting-edge AI automation technology.

2025

ZinClin Beta & ZinTrade Development

ZinClin TLF Code Generator enters beta testing with select clinical programming clients. Simultaneously, development of ZinTrade AI stock analysis tools begins, marking Zinger's expansion into financial technology.

2026

ZinTrade Early Access

ZinTrade stock analysis platform enters early access program. First users gain access to AI-powered signal detection, portfolio risk tools, and backtested trade screening.
