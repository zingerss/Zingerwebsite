About ZinClin & Zinger Software Solutions

Our journey from clinical programming services to AI-powered automation represents 12+ years of industry expertise transformed into scalable innovation.

From Services to Innovation

Founded in 2013, Zinger Software Solutions began as a boutique technical staffing and clinical programming services company. For over a decade, we've provided high-quality programming talent and direct programming services to biotechs and CROs.

We lived the challenges our clients face every day: offshore communication barriers, quality control issues, long turnaround times, and the struggle to maintain competitive costs with local talent.

ZinClin represents our evolution from providing these services manually to automating the expertise our clients have relied on for years through a scalable SaaS platform. We're building a cloud-based software business that serves multiple biotech and CRO clients globally with recurring subscription revenue, rather than custom one-off solutions.
