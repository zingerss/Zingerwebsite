About ZinClin & Zinger Software Solutions

From 12+ years of clinical programming services to Module 1 SDTM automation innovation. Our evolution represents a strategic transition from traditional services to modular AI-powered SaaS solutions.

Our Evolution Story

Zinger Software Solutions was founded in 2013 with a focus on delivering high-quality clinical programming services to the pharmaceutical and biotech industries. Over 12+ years, we've completed hundreds of successful projects, building deep expertise in SDTM, ADaM, and TLF programming.

This extensive experience revealed consistent patterns in clinical programming challenges: repetitive SDTM tasks, offshore quality concerns, and the need for faster, more reliable automation. These insights led to the development of ZinClin, our modular AI platform that transforms this expertise into scalable software solutions.

Starting with Module 1 SDTM automation in Q4 2025, we're transitioning from custom service delivery to modular SaaS subscriptions that provide the same expertise at scale.

Vision & Mission

Our Mission

To transform clinical programming through modular AI automation that enhances human expertise rather than replacing it. Starting with Module 1 SDTM automation, we aim to eliminate repetitive tasks while enabling programmers to focus on complex challenges and innovation.
