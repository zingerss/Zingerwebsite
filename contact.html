Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Module 1 Early Access Program? +

The Module 1 Early Access Program gives you exclusive beta access to ZinClin's SDTM automation capabilities before public launch. You'll test CRF annotation, mapping specifications, and SDTM code generation while providing feedback to shape the final product.

When will Module 1 (SDTM) be available? +

Module 1 beta testing begins Q4 2025 with selected early access participants. General availability is planned for Q1 2026 with full SaaS subscription options and enterprise licensing.

Is ZinClin a modular platform or complete solution? +

ZinClin is designed as a modular SaaS platform. Start with Module 1 (SDTM) for immediate ROI, then add Module 2 (ADaM) and Module 3 (TLF) based on your needs and budget. This approach minimizes risk and maximizes flexibility.

How much will Module 1 SaaS subscriptions cost? +

Module 1 subscription pricing will be announced closer to general availability, with individual, team, and enterprise tiers. Early access participants receive preferential pricing and priority access to future modules.

Can I schedule a demo or strategy consultation? +

Absolutely! Email us at admin@zingersoftwaresolutions.com to schedule a consultation. We can discuss your current SDTM challenges and how Module 1 will address them based on our 12+ years of industry experience.

What about Module 2 (ADaM) and Module 3 (TLF)? +

Module 2 (ADaM) and Module 3 (TLF) development timelines will be determined based on Module 1 feedback and market validation. Early access participants will have priority access and input on future module priorities.
