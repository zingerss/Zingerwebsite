Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Early Access Program? +

The Early Access Program gives you exclusive beta access to ZinClin before its public launch. You'll be able to test the platform, provide feedback, and help shape the final product while getting priority access to new features.

When will ZinClin be available? +

ZinClin is currently in active development. Early access beta testing will begin in Q2 2025, with general availability planned for Q4 2025. Join our early access program to be notified first.

Is ZinClin a SaaS platform or software we install? +

ZinClin is a cloud-based Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform. There's no software to install - you access ZinClin through your web browser with subscription-based pricing and multi-user support for enterprise organizations.

How much will ZinClin SaaS subscriptions cost? +

SaaS subscription pricing will be announced closer to launch, with multiple tiers including individual, team, and enterprise licensing. Our goal is to provide significant ROI compared to offshore alternatives. Early access participants will receive preferential pricing.

Can I schedule a demo or consultation? +

Absolutely! Email us at admin@zingersoftwaresolutions.com to schedule a consultation. While the product is in development, we can discuss your current challenges and how ZinClin will address them based on our 12+ years of industry experience.
